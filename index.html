University Offer Predictor

A predictor for predicting probability of receiving offers from universities, based on high school exam results.

A Python based predictor that is trained on a few years of exam results of Graduates. Comes in two versions, one trained with convolutional neural network, one trained with recurrent neural network. Works by regressing the exam results to probabilities.

Final Year Project

Recipe for a Perfect Recipe: BERT Piloted Error Detection for Cooking Recipes

A project to utilise BERT models in finding cooking recipe errors.

To minimise frustrating moments and unwanted cooking results, I have trained multiple feasible BERT based large language models in finding a number of errors in cooking recipes. This project explores large language model finetuning processes, hyperparameter tuning, data processing, transfer learning and much more.
